About Us

Global Logistics Group, a subsidiary of GASSSTS, Inc. Holding Company, specializes in international oil and gas trade through secure buyer-seller transactions. Our experienced management ensures efficient documentation using SWIFT communications and secure fuel shipping from origin to destination.

Founded in 2003, our journey began with a proposal for a floating LNG terminal in the Gulf of Mexico. We later engaged with Qatar Energy leadership in Washington D.C. to discuss LNG business relations. Today, we focus on exporting to global off-takers and supporting domestic fuel initiatives across the U.S.

As demand for fuel and natural gas evolves, we are committed to offering adaptable solutions for storage, logistics, and international shipping, fully compliant with industry standards.

What We Do

We facilitate international fuel trade, covering the entire value chain from refinery to end buyer. Our team manages logistics, documentation, and product storage for smooth delivery across global shipping routes.

Key Considerations:

  • Due Diligence: Vet all parties to prevent fraud.
  • Clear Communication: Maintain transparency throughout the process.
  • Legal Expertise: Employ counsel with international trade experience.
  • Trusted Third-Parties: Use verified inspectors, shipping agents, and banks.
  • Risk Management: Mitigate risks like price volatility, geopolitical shifts, and shipping delays.
  • Incoterms: Define responsibilities and costs clearly (e.g., FOB, CIF).

Our Main Products

We offer a diverse portfolio of fuel and energy products:

  • Petroleum: Crude oil refined into fuels and industrial chemicals.
  • EN590 Diesel: Ultra-low sulfur diesel meeting Euro 4/5 standards.
  • Jet Fuel (Jet A/Jet A-1): Premium aviation fuel for turbine engines.
  • Naphtha: Flammable liquid for gasoline blending and petrochemical production.
  • ULSD: Ultra-Low Sulfur Diesel for reduced emissions and cleaner combustion.
  • D2 Diesel: Low-sulfur gasoil ideal for industrial and vehicle use.

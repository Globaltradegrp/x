What We Do

Global Logistics Group facilitates international fuel product trade, from refinery to the ultimate buyer, involving complex procedures for successful and compliant transactions[cite: 9]. These procedures often vary based on agreed-upon Incoterms (e.g., FOB - Free On Board, CIF - Cost, Insurance, and Freight), the specific product, and the countries involved[cite: 10].

Key Considerations for Success:

  • **Due Diligence:** Thoroughly vet all parties (buyer, seller, intermediaries) to prevent fraudulent schemes[cite: 11].
  • **Clear Communication:** Maintain open and transparent communication throughout the entire process[cite: 12].
  • **Legal Expertise:** Engage experienced legal counsel specializing in international trade and energy law[cite: 13].
  • **Reputable Third-Party Services:** Utilize reliable and independent inspection companies, shipping agents, and banks[cite: 14].
  • **Risk Management:** Understand and mitigate various risks, including price volatility, geopolitical instability, shipping delays, and quality discrepancies[cite: 15].
  • **Incoterms:** Clearly define and understand the responsibilities, costs, and risks associated with the chosen Incoterms[cite: 16].

This detailed process highlights the intricate nature of international fuel product transactions, emphasizing the need for precision, trust, and adherence to established protocols[cite: 17].

Our Main Products

We supply a range of essential fuel products:

  • **Petroleum:** Naturally occurring crude oil, refined into fuels and chemicals[cite: 19]. Available in various forms like liquid crude, bitumen, or semi-solid mixed with sand, used in numerous applications[cite: 19].
  • **EN590 Diesel:** Automotive diesel meeting European Union emission standards (Euro 4/5), with ultra-low sulfur content, ensuring reduced environmental impact and enhanced engine performance[cite: 20].
  • **Jet Fuel (Jet A/Jet A-1):** High-quality aviation fuel for turbine engines; Jet A-1 withstands cold climates, while Jet A is mostly used in the U.S., offering optimal performance and international standards[cite: 21].
  • **Naphtha:** A volatile, flammable liquid used in gasoline blending, petrochemical production, and as a raw material in producing plastics, chemicals, and industrial solvents[cite: 22].
  • **ULSD (Ultra-Low Sulfur Diesel):** Cleaner diesel fuel with minimal sulfur content, enabling advanced emission technologies, especially in Europe and North America, to reduce particulate matter in diesel engines[cite: 23].
  • **D2 Diesel:** A low-sulfur gasoil distillate used in diesel engines; efficient combustion makes it ideal for vehicles and industrial use, particularly in cold weather with added winter-specific additives[cite: 24].
